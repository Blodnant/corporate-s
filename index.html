About

Corporate Suite presents Fortfield;
a collection of exclusive and stylish one-bedroom serviced apartments, nestled in the

If you are in need of a corporate retreat or simply an indulgent getaway with all the ease and comfort of home, Corporate Suite is at your service.

Carefully curated for the discerning traveller

Each impressive apartment has been designed to make your stay as enjoyable as possible. Whether you're here for a short visit or a longer period, you’ll find everything you need, so that you can focus on what matters most. From high-speed Wi-Fi to fully equipped kitchens, each feature has been thoughtfully chosen to support both work and relaxation.
